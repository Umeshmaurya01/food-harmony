Fact Check

Breaking Down Nutrition Myths

Separating fact from fiction in the world of nutrition

Carbs Make You Fat

MYTH

Not all carbohydrates are created equal. The type and quantity matter most.

The Truth:

  • Complex carbs provide essential energy
  • Whole grains support weight management
  • Fiber helps maintain fullness

Quality carbs are crucial for brain function and athletic performance

Choose Wisely:
Better Options
  • Whole grain bread
  • Brown rice
  • Sweet potatoes
Limit These
  • White bread
  • Sugary cereals
  • Processed snacks

All Fats Are Bad

MYTH

Healthy fats are essential for proper body function and nutrient absorption.

The Truth:

  • Omega-3s support brain health
  • Healthy fats aid vitamin absorption
  • Essential for hormone production

Good fats can help reduce inflammation and support heart health

Types of Fats:
Healthy
  • Avocados
  • Olive oil
  • Nuts & seeds
Unhealthy
  • Trans fats
  • Processed oils
  • Fried foods

Skipping Meals Helps Weight Loss

MYTH

Regular meals help maintain stable blood sugar and prevent overeating.

The Truth:

  • Maintains metabolic rate
  • Prevents energy crashes
  • Supports portion control

Regular meals help maintain stable energy levels throughout the day
